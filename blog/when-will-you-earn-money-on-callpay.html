When will you earn money on Callpay?

So you have downloaded Callpay, registered on this App too, even Ads are also appearing after each incoming call but you are not earning money, right?
We understand your problem and that is why here we have presented the solutions to the issues you are facing.
Earning on Callpay is very easy. It credits money in your wallet just for watching Ads, clicking on them and making purchases through them.

How to understand which are situation 1 type Ads?

  • General informative type Ads that awares your about any brand or product.
  • No download/ install/ register/ order now button.
  • Watch the whole Ad
  • Check your Callpay wallet after some time. Money will automatically get credited there.

How much can you earn from Watch only Ads?

  • For each watch only Ads, you will earn Re.03 to Re.1.

How to understand which are situation 2 type Ads?

  • General informative type Ads.
  • These Ads pay just for clicking on it.
  • Check your Callpay wallet after some time. Money will automatically get credited there.

How much can you earn from Watch and Click only Ads?

  • For each watch and click only Ads, you will earn Re.1 to Rs.4.

How to understand which are situation 3 type Ads?

  • On these Ads you will see a download now or install now button.
  • After you click on this Ad, it will redirect you to some other page.
  • On that page you will see an install or download button.
  • You have to download/ install that App.
  • After finishing the download, check your Callpay wallet. Money will be added there.

How much can you earn from Watch-Click-Install Ads?

  • For each watch and click only Ads, you will earn Re.1 to Rs.4.

How to understand which are situation 4 type Ads?

  • On these types of Ads also you will see a download or install now button.
  • After you click on this Ad, it will redirect you to some other page.
  • On that page you will see an install or download button.
  • You have to download/ install that App.
  • After finishing the download, you have to open the downloaded App and install it properly allowing all the required permissions.
  • Now you need to register on the App with all the required information.

How much can you earn from Watch-Click-Install-Register Ads?

  • For each watch-click-install-register Ads, you will earn Rs.4 to Rs.10.

How to understand which are situation 5 type Ads?

  • On these types of Ads you will see various deals, for example- 50% off, 70% off, etc.
  • After you click on this Ad, it will redirect you to some other page.
  • If the Ad shown App is already installed in your mobile, then you just click on the Ad and make a purchase through the same link.
  • If the App is not on your device then on the redirected page you will see an install or download button.
  • You have to download/ install that App.
  • After finishing the download, you have to open the downloaded App and install it properly allowing all the required permissions.
  • Now you need to register on the App with all the required information.
  • After you are done registering on it, you have to make a purchase on through the same link.

How much can you earn from Watch-Click-Install-Register-Purchase Ads?

  • For each watch-click-install-register-purchase Ads, you will earn Rs.4 to Rs.15.

Important facts to remember:

  • You earn cash depending on which type of Ad is appearing on your device
  • What are your actions with the Ads.
  • To be on the profitable side, always click on Ads that appear.
  • To earn money, you need to perform all the tasks that the redirected page shows you.

Ads are coming on your Phone through Callpay but you are not earning money.

You will not earn money if:

  1. The Ad is not situation 1 type and you don’t click on the Ad.
  2. When you are redirected to a page to download an App but the App is pre-installed in your device.
  3. You are already a user of the Advertised App.
  4. You do not register on the App with all the required information, or you skip any step.
  5. You install and register on an App but do not avail the Ad shown discount to make purchases.
  6. Your phone doesn’t have good network coverage.
